Relativity.

The passage of time is faster for your face than it is for your feet (assuming you're standing). Einstein's theory of relativity states that the closer you are to the center of the Earth, the slower time will pass - and this has been measured. For example, at the top of Mount Everest, a year is about 15 microseconds shorter than at sea level.

What is time management?.

Time management is knowing how to enjoy 100% of every moment. When you are on working hours, WORK! When you are out of these hours, ENJOY intensely with your family, friends and sometimes allow yourself to donate time just for you.
